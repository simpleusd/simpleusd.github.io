The Metronotes XMN Token was created to allow Open Participation in Development of the Metronotes Network. The Metronotes Network is a Network Services Candidate for Tetcoin. The Platform consists of Metrochain, Metroswap, VistaDAO, Vega, and Xynthe. As components of Metronotes are released, holders of XMN may burn their tokens in exchange for holdings of respective protocol. However, anyone can burn XMN at any time for any reason. Burn Away.

The Original Metronotes Blockchain is currently bootstrapped on the Bitcoin Network as a protocol for decentralized exchange. This occurred when the Metronotes Team Burned Bitcoin in 2015. This dependency on a Proof of Work Layer is counterproductive to the goals of the Metronotes Project. Holders of the Original Metronotes XMN may convert their stake equally to the Metronotes Smart Contract at any time. Metrochain is being built on Tetcoin and is replacing the Metronotes Blockchain in the Metronotes Platform.
